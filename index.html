Mask Recipes
Step-by-step guide for each mask
🟡Ubtan, Every Day
1
Take 2 spoons of besan (gram flour)
2
Add tiny pinch of haldi. Too much = yellow skin!
3
Add enough milk to make thick paste. Mix well.
4
Apply on face, neck, arms & body before shower
5
Wait 20 minutes until it dries
6
Rub gently with wet hands (light scrub), then shower
🟠Yogurt Mask, Most Nights
1
Take 2 spoons of plain dahi. Nothing else!
2
Apply on face & arms after Isha
3
Wait 15 minutes
4
Wash with cold water, then apply aloe vera
🍋Lemon + Honey (Day 4, 7, 10, 13 Only)
1
Mix 1 spoon honey + 4 to 5 drops lemon
2
Apply on face & arms. Wait 15 minutes.
3
Wash with cold water
4
⚠️ Only at night. Never go in sun after lemon!
5
Then apply aloe vera gel and sleep
🟢Aloe Vera, Every Night
1
Buy Aloe Vera gel from pharmacy (Rs. 150 to 300)
2
Apply on face & arms after night mask
3
Do NOT wash. Sleep with it all night.
4
Wash normally in the morning
